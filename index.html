Habit Attacker Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 1, 2025

Your Privacy Matters

Habit Attacker is designed with your privacy as a priority. We believe your personal data should stay personal.

Data Collection

We do not collect any data. All information you enter into Habit Attacker—including your tasks, trackers, reports, and sobriety clocks—is stored locally on your device only.

Data Storage

Your data never leaves your device. There are no servers, no cloud storage, and no accounts required. If you delete the app, your data is deleted with it.

Third-Party Services

Habit Attacker offers optional Google Sign-In for local user identification only. If you choose to sign in with Google, we do not store or transmit your Google account information to any server. This is used solely to identify your local profile on your device.

Analytics

We do not use any analytics or tracking tools. We have no way to see how you use the app.

Children's Privacy

Habit Attacker does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we will post the new version here with an updated date.
